Design Manager

Ahmd Saladin

I'm a multidisciplinary designer shaping visual experiences at the intersection of beauty, clarity, and intention. From digital products to cinematic posters, I design not just for function, but for feeling. With a strong foundation in Product Design, UI/UX, Graphic Design, and Poster Art, I bring a deep sense of narrative and emotional intelligence into every project. My work blends glassmorphic futurism, minimalist elegance, and symbolic storytelling to craft interfaces and visuals that are both striking and intuitive. Whether you're building a new brand, launching an app, or curating a visual identity, I design with precision, purpose, and poetry.

POSTER DESIGN

I create cinematic, concept-driven posters that translate emotion into form. Whether for film, music, or culture, each piece balances composition, storytelling, and symbolic visual language.

BRANDING

Beyond a logo, I build full visual systems: typography, color, tone, and layout — everything a brand needs to move, speak, and feel coherent across mediums.

PRODUCT & PACKAGING DESIGN

I give physical form to brands through tactile, minimalist product and packaging design — merging utility with visual story, material sensitivity, and shelf presence.

UI/UX Design

I design digital experiences that are intuitive, elegant, and human-centered. From wireframes to final interfaces, I create flows and visuals that feel as natural as they are functional.
